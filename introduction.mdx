## Why Not Just a Chatbot?

There is an important difference between a generic chatbot and a Woblar AI Agent, and it matters for your business.

A generic chatbot follows a fixed decision tree. It can only respond to exact matches, breaks the moment a customer phrases something unexpectedly, and has no knowledge of your business beyond what you manually hard-code into it. It cannot take action — it can only display text.

A Woblar AI Agent is an autonomous system trained exclusively on your business: your pricing, your product catalogue, your tone of voice, your booking rules. It understands natural language, handles conversations that go off-script, and — critically — it *acts*. It qualifies a lead and logs it to your CRM. It checks a real calendar and writes a confirmed booking. It resolves a support query without escalating. It connects to your POS, ERP, or accounting software so that conversation outcomes flow directly into the systems that run your business — rather than sitting in front of them as a dead end.

## Who Is Woblar For?

Woblar is designed for South African SMBs that deal with high inbound communication volume and cannot afford to staff a 24/7 response team. If customers contact you to ask questions, book appointments, or get support — and those conversations are currently falling through the cracks — Woblar was built for you.

The service is a strong fit for:

- **Service businesses** — medical and dental practices, beauty salons, physiotherapists, consultancies, law firms, and any business where appointment scheduling is core to revenue.
- **Retail** — online and physical stores that field constant questions about stock, pricing, delivery, and returns.
- **Hospitality** — guesthouses, lodges, restaurants, and event venues handling booking enquiries across multiple channels.
- **Logistics and field services** — businesses coordinating pickups, deliveries, or site visits where scheduling accuracy is critical.
- **Financial services** — brokers, advisors, and insurers where fast lead response and compliant communication matter.

All data handling complies with POPIA (the Protection of Personal Information Act), so your customers' details are managed responsibly and lawfully.
