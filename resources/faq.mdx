This page answers the questions most South African businesses ask before getting started with Woblar. Whether you're curious about setup time, data safety, or how your AI Agent actually works, you'll find a straightforward answer below.

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="What exactly is Woblar?">
    Woblar is a managed AI Agent service. We build, train, and run a custom AI Agent for your business — so you're not just getting a chatbot, you're getting a digital team member that takes real actions. Your Woblar Agent can book appointments, qualify leads, resolve support queries, and handle order enquiries. It's trained exclusively on your business — your pricing, your products, your tone of voice — not a generic template.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to go live?">
    Typically **14 days** from your first call with the Woblar team. The exact timeline depends on how quickly you can share your business materials — things like your FAQs, pricing lists, product catalogue, and channel access credentials. The faster we can get those from you, the faster your agent goes live.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need any technical skills?">
    None at all. Woblar handles every aspect of building, deploying, and maintaining your AI Agent. You provide the business content and context — we take care of everything else, from training the model to connecting it to your chosen channels. There's nothing for you to install or configure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which channels does my agent run on?">
    Your agent can run on **WhatsApp**, **web chat**, and **Instagram DMs**. Woblar can deploy on one channel or all three simultaneously — whichever makes the most sense for how your customers prefer to reach you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my business data safe?">
    Yes. Woblar complies with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). We never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. Our AI model providers — Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google — are contractually prohibited from using your data to train their models. For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](/resources/privacy).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my agent trained on other businesses' data?">
    No. Your agent is trained exclusively on your business — your pricing, products, tone of voice, and FAQs. It will never reference a competitor's information, and no other client's agent is trained on your data. What you share with Woblar stays within your engagement.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after my agent goes live?">
    Woblar continuously monitors real conversations and tunes your agent on a weekly basis. Every interaction provides signal that helps us improve accuracy, tone, and coverage. You don't need to manage this — your Woblar team handles all ongoing optimisation on your behalf.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my agent doesn't know the answer?">
    Rather than guessing, your agent escalates the conversation to your human team. You define the escalation criteria during onboarding — for example, queries outside a certain topic area, or customers who explicitly ask to speak to a person. Your customers always get a helpful response, even when the agent reaches its limits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What industries does Woblar serve?">
    Woblar works with any South African SMB that communicates with customers. Common sectors include:

    - **Service businesses** — clinics, salons, consultancies, and repair services
    - **Retail** — both brick-and-mortar and e-commerce stores
    - **Hospitality** — guesthouses, restaurants, and event venues
    - **Logistics** — courier and delivery operations
    - **Finance** — brokers, advisors, and short-term lenders
    - **E-commerce** — online-first brands needing 24/7 customer support

    If your business has customers asking questions, Woblar can help.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is Woblar priced?">
    Fees are set out in individual service agreements, quoted in ZAR and excluding VAT. Pricing depends on the scope of your engagement — the number of channels, the complexity of the workflows, and any integrations required. To get a proposal tailored to your business, contact us at [hello@woblar.com](mailto:hello@woblar.com).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my AI Agent connect to my existing business tools?">
    Yes. Woblar can integrate your AI Agent with the tools your business already uses — for example, your booking system, CRM, or order management platform. Integration scope is agreed during the onboarding process and documented in your service agreement. If you have a specific system in mind, mention it when you reach out and we'll let you know what's possible.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a self-serve portal or dashboard I can log into?">
    Not currently. Woblar is a fully managed service — your Woblar team handles all configuration, monitoring, and optimisation on your behalf. If you have questions about your agent's performance, you can reach us directly at [hello@woblar.com](mailto:hello@woblar.com).
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
