Woblar is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA, Act 4 of 2013). This policy explains what data we collect when you visit woblar.com or engage our services, how we use it, who we share it with, and what rights you have over your own information.

<Note>
  This policy is issued by **Alien Intelligence (Pty) Ltd t/a Woblar**, Registration No. 2023/965677/07. It takes effect on **25 April 2026** and replaces any earlier privacy notices published by Woblar.
</Note>

## Information we collect

We collect personal information in three distinct contexts:

**Website visitors.** When you browse woblar.com, we collect anonymised analytics data through Google Analytics. IP anonymisation is enabled, which means we never store your full IP address. This data tells us which pages are popular and how visitors navigate the site — it cannot be used to identify you personally.

**Contact and enquiry leads.** When you fill in a contact form, request a demo, or send us a message, we collect your name, email address, business name, and the content of your message. We use this information to respond to your enquiry and, where you've given consent, to follow up with relevant information about Woblar's services.

**Client end-customer data.** When you engage Woblar to build and run an AI Agent for your business, your customers may share personal information through that agent (for example, a name and phone number when booking an appointment). Woblar processes this data solely on your instruction, as an operator under POPIA. We do not use it for our own purposes, and we do not combine it with data from other clients.

## How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to your enquiries and provide the services you've requested
- Improve the woblar.com website based on anonymised usage patterns
- Deliver, monitor, and improve AI Agent services for our clients
- Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

The legal bases we rely on are contractual necessity (to deliver services), legitimate interest (to respond to enquiries and improve our offering), consent (for marketing communications), and legal obligation (where the law requires us to retain or disclose information).

<Info>
  We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your prior consent.
</Info>

## Your rights under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the personal information Woblar holds about you
- **Correction** — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion** — request that we delete your personal information, subject to our legal retention obligations
- **Objection** — object to the processing of your personal information on grounds relating to your particular situation
- **Complain** — lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa at [inforegulator.org.za](https://www.inforegulator.org.za)

To exercise any of these rights, email our Information Officer at [hello@woblar.com](mailto:hello@woblar.com). We will respond within 30 days.

## Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary:

- **Enquiry and lead data** — up to 24 months from the date of your last interaction with us
- **Client engagement data** — for the duration of the contract plus 5 years thereafter, in line with standard business record-keeping obligations
- **Anonymised website analytics** — retained indefinitely, as this data cannot be linked back to any individual

## Security

Woblar takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. These measures include HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit, access controls that limit who can view personal information internally, and the use of certified infrastructure providers (Vercel, AWS, Google Cloud).

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe your personal information has been compromised, contact us immediately at [hello@woblar.com](mailto:hello@woblar.com).
