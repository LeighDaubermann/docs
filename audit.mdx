The **Woblar AI Audit** is a 10-minute diagnostic tool designed specifically for South African small and medium-sized business owners. Instead of dealing in high-level AI buzzwords, the audit conducts a practical analysis of your current operations, communication channels, software tools, and bottlenecks to generate a personalized **AI Readiness & Opportunity Map**.

The audit helps you identify the lowest-risk, highest-yield processes to automate first, giving you a clear roadmap to reclaim your time, reduce administrative overhead, and scale operations.

## What You Receive: The Audit Report

After completing the intake, our custom AI analyzer generates a detailed operational blueprint containing:

### 1. AI Readiness Score
A structural assessment of your business's data, software integration potential, and operational flexibility. This score helps set realistic expectations for what can be automated immediately versus what requires foundation preparation.

### 2. Custom AI Opportunity Map
A matrix of tailored AI use cases designed for your business model, categorized by:
- **Low-Hanging Fruit:** Easy integrations with immediate high impact (e.g., booking automation).
- **Strategic Wins:** More complex workflows that unlock significant leverage (e.g., custom CRM-connected agents).
- **Long-Term Bets:** Advanced systems to build toward as your data foundations mature.

### 3. Recommended Pilot Blueprint
A complete blueprint for a low-risk, high-return starting project. This proposal defines:
- **Target Channels & Integration points:** (e.g., WhatsApp Business connected to Google Calendar).
- **Core Knowledge scope:** What the pilot agent needs to know.
- **Key Metrics for success:** How we will measure the return on investment (hours saved, response times).
- **Expected ROI projection:** Simple payback estimates based on your hourly rates and bottleneck costs.

### 1. Take the Audit
Go to the [Online AI Audit](https://woblar.com/audit) on the main website, sign in, and complete the 10-step diagnostic. You can save your progress and return to it at any time.

Your report is generated instantly and saved to your Woblar Profile. It includes custom flowcharts, return on investment estimates, and your pilot blueprint.

### 3. Book a Founder Review
Once your audit is ready, you can book a highly focused, 20-minute review call with Leigh. Because we already have your exact business profile and report, we bypass the generic pitch and jump straight into refining your pilot scope and discussing practical next steps.
