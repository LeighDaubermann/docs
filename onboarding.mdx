Getting started with Woblar does not require any technical knowledge, any software installation, or any project management on your part. Woblar's team runs the entire onboarding process from start to finish. Your role is to share what you know about your business — your products, your customers, your common questions — and Woblar turns that knowledge into a fully operational AI Agent. From your first call to a live agent handling real customer conversations typically takes 14 days.

## Your Four-Phase Onboarding Journey

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1 — Understand Your World">
    Woblar begins with a structured discovery session where the team sits down with you to map out exactly where you are losing time and money in your daily communication. Together you walk through how customers currently reach you, what they ask most often, where conversations stall or drop off, and what a successful customer interaction looks like for your business.

    You share your products or services, pricing, common customer questions, booking or sales workflows, and any existing tools you use — calendar software, CRM, POS, ERP, accounting software, or WhatsApp Business account. Nothing technical is required from you — this is a conversation, not a form to fill in. By the end of Phase 1, Woblar has a complete picture of your business and a clear blueprint for your AI Agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 2 — Train Your AI Agent">
    With your business mapped, Woblar builds and trains your custom AI Agent. Your pricing, business rules, FAQs, product or service catalogue, and tone of voice are all fed into the agent's knowledge base. The agent is trained exclusively on your business — it speaks your language, reflects your brand personality, and answers questions the way you would answer them yourself.

    This phase also includes integration work: connecting the agent to your calendar for real booking capability, linking it to your CRM if applicable, and — where relevant — integrating with your POS, ERP, or accounting software so that outcomes flow directly into the systems your business already runs on. Woblar also sets up the escalation logic that determines when a conversation should be handed to a human, then tests the agent thoroughly against realistic customer scenarios before advancing to deployment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 3 — Flip The Switch">
    Once the agent passes testing, Woblar connects it to the channels your customers already use. Your WhatsApp Business number, your website chat widget, and your Instagram DMs are activated in sequence. From the moment each channel goes live, every inbound message receives an immediate, accurate response — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

    The pressure lifts from your team immediately. After-hours enquiries get answered. Appointment slots fill without phone calls. Repetitive questions stop landing in your inbox. Your business is effectively awake at all times, even when you are not.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 4 — Watch It Grow">
    Going live is not the end — it is the beginning of an ongoing optimisation loop. Woblar continuously monitors your agent's real customer conversations, identifying gaps in its knowledge, questions it could handle better, and new topics your customers raise that were not part of the original training.

    On a regular cadence, Woblar updates the agent's training based on this real-world data. Every conversation makes the agent smarter and more accurate. Over time, your AI Agent becomes an increasingly precise reflection of your business and an increasingly capable front line for your customer communications.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Need to Provide

Woblar handles all the technical work, but the agent's quality depends on the quality of the business knowledge you share. Before or during your first session, gather the following:

- **Product or service descriptions** — what you offer, how it works, and any key details customers need to know before buying or booking.
- **Pricing** — your standard rates, packages, or pricing tiers. If pricing varies, explain how it varies.
- **Frequently asked questions** — the questions your team answers repeatedly every day. The more you can provide, the faster Phase 2 moves.
- **Sample conversations** *(if available)* — real WhatsApp threads, email exchanges, or chat logs that show how your customers actually communicate and what they typically ask.
- **Access to your WhatsApp Business number** — Woblar needs to connect the agent to your existing number. If you do not yet have a WhatsApp Business account, Woblar will guide you through setting one up.

<Tip>
  Start collecting your most common customer questions now — even a rough list in a notes app or spreadsheet speeds up Phase 2 significantly and gets you to a live agent faster.
</Tip>

## Timeline

The standard onboarding journey runs as follows:

| Phase | Activity | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| Phase 1 | Discovery and business mapping | Days 1–3 |
| Phase 2 | AI Agent training and integration | Days 4–10 |
| Phase 3 | Channel activation and go-live | Days 11–12 |
| Phase 4 | Monitoring and first tune | Day 14 onwards |

Most clients have a live agent handling real customer conversations within 14 days of their first call with Woblar. Complex integrations (multiple CRMs, custom booking systems, or high product volume) may extend this slightly — Woblar will set a clear expectation during Phase 1.

<Note>
  POPIA compliance is built into the process. Woblar structures data collection, storage, and processing in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act from day one. You do not need a separate compliance review before going live.
</Note>
