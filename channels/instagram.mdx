The problem is that DMs arrive at all hours, and slow responses mean lost business. Your Woblar AI Agent connects directly to your Instagram account and handles every incoming DM automatically, so no message ever goes unanswered.

## How Activation Works

Woblar connects and configures the Instagram integration for you. The process is fast and requires minimal input from your side.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Your Instagram Business Account">
    Grant Woblar the permissions needed to connect your Instagram Business account. This is done through a secure authorisation flow — you remain in full control of your account at all times.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar Configures the Agent">
    Woblar sets up the agent with your business context — services, pricing, booking process, tone of voice — so it responds accurately and on-brand from day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Agent Begins Responding Automatically">
    Once configured, your agent starts handling incoming DMs immediately. Every new message gets an instant, intelligent reply — no manual effort required from your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Your Instagram account must be set up as a **Business** or **Creator** account — not a personal account — to enable automated DM responses. If your account is currently personal, switching to a Business or Creator account is free and takes only a few minutes in the Instagram app settings.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Update your Instagram bio to drive more DMs to your agent — something like: *"Message us for bookings & enquiries — instant reply 24/7."* The clearer you make it that your DMs are monitored and responsive, the more enquiries you'll receive.
</Tip>
