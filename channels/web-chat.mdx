Your website already generates intent — visitors arrive because they searched for what you offer, clicked your ad, or heard about you from someone. The web chat agent's job is to convert that intent into a lead before the visitor loses interest and leaves. With your Woblar AI Agent embedded on your site, every visitor gets an instant, intelligent response the moment they have a question.

Your agent works as a 24/7 front-desk presence on your website, handling the full visitor journey from first hello to confirmed booking.

## Speed-to-Lead: Why Response Time Matters

When a visitor reaches out on your website and waits more than 30 minutes for a reply, the odds of qualifying that lead drop dramatically. Research shows that responding within **5 minutes** makes you **21 times more likely to qualify** a lead than responding after half an hour — because by then, they've already moved on to a competitor.

Your Woblar web chat agent eliminates that wait entirely. The moment a visitor asks a question or submits their details, the agent responds — capturing interest at its peak and keeping the conversation moving toward a booked appointment or confirmed sale.

## How It's Set Up

Setup is straightforward and handled almost entirely by Woblar. You only need to add a single line of code to your website.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Woblar Builds and Trains the Agent">
    Woblar uses your business content — services, FAQs, pricing, tone of voice — to build and train an agent that represents your brand accurately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar Provides the Embed Snippet">
    Once the agent is ready, Woblar gives you a small embed snippet — a short piece of code — to place on your website.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the Snippet to Your Site">
    You or your web developer paste the snippet into your website's HTML, typically in the footer or before the closing `</body>` tag. This works with any website platform — WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, custom-built sites, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The Agent Goes Live Immediately">
    As soon as the snippet is added, the chat widget appears on your site and your agent begins responding to visitors in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The embed snippet is a single line of code — no complex integration, no developer specialisation, and no ongoing maintenance required on your end.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Enable the web chat agent on your highest-traffic pages first — typically your home page, services page, and pricing page. These are where visitors have the most questions and where early engagement has the greatest impact on lead capture.
</Tip>
