## How Activation Works

Woblar handles everything on the technical side. You don't need to configure the WhatsApp Business API yourself — Woblar does it for you.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share Your WhatsApp Business Number">
    Provide Woblar with your existing WhatsApp Business number (your 082 number). No new SIM card or number is needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar Completes API Verification">
    Woblar handles the WhatsApp Business API verification process with Meta on your behalf — including all documentation and business verification steps.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar Deploys and Trains the Agent">
    Your agent is configured with your business context — services, tone, FAQs, booking logic — and deployed on your number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You Go Live">
    Your agent starts responding to incoming messages immediately. From this point, no enquiry goes unanswered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your existing WhatsApp Business number is used throughout this process — no new number or SIM card is required.
</Note>

## The Missed-Call Problem

In South Africa, a missed call on your 082 number is revenue walking out the door. The caller moves on — to a competitor, to Google, to someone who picks up. Your Woblar AI Agent solves this by sending an automatic WhatsApp follow-up message to every number that calls and doesn't get through.

Instead of losing that lead, your agent opens a conversation: it greets the caller, asks what they need, and begins qualifying them — all before they've had time to try someone else. Every missed call becomes a second chance to win the business.

<Info>
  WhatsApp messages are transmitted through Meta's WhatsApp Business Platform. Woblar ensures that your end-customers are aware they are interacting with a business-operated agent, in line with WhatsApp's terms and South Africa's POPIA requirements.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Let your customers know your WhatsApp is now active 24/7. Update your business card, email signature, Google Business Profile, and social media bio with your WhatsApp number — the more touchpoints you activate, the more leads your agent captures.
</Tip>
