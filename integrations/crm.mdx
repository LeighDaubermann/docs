## How It Works

Woblar manages the entire API connection between your agent and your CRM — you don't need to configure anything yourself. When a prospect enters the funnel, your agent evaluates their budget, maps friction points, and decides whether they qualify. The moment a lead meets your qualification criteria, a new record is created in your CRM automatically. When the agent books a meeting, it appears in the closer's calendar instantly — no manual handoff required.

Woblar's team handles all integration logic, field mapping, and testing before you go live. If your CRM uses custom fields or a non-standard pipeline structure, Woblar configures the integration to match your exact setup.

## Setup Process

Woblar handles every step of the integration. Here's what to expect:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Share your CRM platform and access credentials with Woblar">
    Let the Woblar team know which CRM you use and provide the API credentials or access token needed to connect.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar configures the API connection">
    Woblar's engineers map your pipeline stages, custom fields, and lead ownership rules to the agent's output data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar tests the lead-to-CRM flow">
    The team runs end-to-end tests to confirm that leads, transcripts, qualification scores, and booked meetings all land correctly in your CRM.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live — leads start flowing automatically">
    Once testing passes, your agent goes live. Every qualified lead and booked meeting is logged in your CRM from that moment forward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  No changes to your existing CRM setup are required. Woblar adds leads and meeting records alongside your existing pipeline — your current workflows, views, and automations stay exactly as they are.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Define your lead qualification criteria clearly during onboarding — the agent uses them to decide what gets logged as a hot lead versus a general enquiry. The more specific you are, the cleaner your pipeline stays.
</Tip>
