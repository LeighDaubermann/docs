Your AI Booking Agent needs live access to your calendar to confirm available slots and lock in appointments — the calendar integration makes this possible without you ever manually checking your diary. Customers get instant confirmations at any hour of the day, and your schedule stays accurate without any intervention from your team.

## How the Calendar Integration Works

Once connected, your agent handles the entire booking process from first message to confirmed appointment. Here's what happens automatically:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Agent reads your live availability">
    The agent queries your calendar in real time so it only offers slots that are genuinely open — no double-bookings, no awkward manual corrections.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer selects and confirms a slot">
    The agent presents available times within the conversation — on WhatsApp, web chat, or Instagram — and confirms the booking the moment the customer chooses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calendar invite sent to the customer">
    A calendar invite goes out to the customer immediately, with all relevant details included. The slot is blocked in your calendar at the same time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reminder sent 24 hours before the appointment">
    The agent sends an automated reminder to the customer the day before, reducing no-shows without any action from your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deposit Collection

If your business requires a deposit to secure a booking, the agent handles that too. As part of the booking conversation, the agent requests payment before finalising the appointment — no separate payment link, no chasing. Woblar sets up the payment gateway connection as part of your onboarding, so the entire flow from first message to paid, confirmed booking happens without any human involvement.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="In-conversation payment request" icon="credit-card">
    The agent asks for the deposit naturally within the booking flow — customers pay without leaving the chat.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Booking confirmed only on payment" icon="lock">
    The slot is only blocked in your calendar once the deposit is received, eliminating unpaid no-shows.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Setup Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm your calendar software with Woblar">
    Let the Woblar team know which calendar platform you use so they can prepare the right integration.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share calendar access (read/write permissions)">
    Grant Woblar the read and write access needed to check availability and create booking events on your behalf.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define your available hours, buffer times, and service durations">
    Tell Woblar your working hours, how long each appointment type takes, and any buffer time you want between bookings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar connects and tests the booking flow">
    The team runs end-to-end tests to confirm the agent reads availability correctly, books accurately, and sends confirmations as expected.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live — customers start booking autonomously">
    Once testing is complete, your agent handles bookings 24/7 with zero manual involvement from your side.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  The agent only books within your defined available hours. It won't create conflicts, ignore buffers, or double-book — your calendar rules are enforced automatically.
</Info>

<Tip>
  Set buffer times between appointments directly in your calendar settings — the agent reads and respects them automatically. This is the simplest way to ensure you always have transition time between clients.
</Tip>
