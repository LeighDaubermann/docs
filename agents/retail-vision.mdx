## Privacy and Compliance

The Vision Engine is built from the ground up to be compliant with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Computer vision processing happens on edge nodes installed at your premises — footage never leaves your site. The system does not perform facial recognition and does not capture or store any biometric identifiers. It extracts only anonymous movement vectors: directional flow data that tells you how customers move without identifying who they are. No personal information is processed, stored, or transmitted.
