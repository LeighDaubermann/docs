Every lead you generate costs you money — paid ads, SEO, referrals, trade shows. Yet the single biggest variable in whether that lead converts has nothing to do with your product: it's how fast you respond. Research consistently shows that responding within five minutes makes you 21 times more likely to qualify a prospect than responding after 30 minutes. At 8:45 PM, when your sales team is offline and a prospect submits your contact form, that window slams shut in seconds. Your Woblar Lead Capture Agent keeps it open — responding instantly, qualifying intelligently, and booking meetings into your CRM calendar without any human intervention.

## How the Lead Capture Agent Works

Your agent runs continuously across every channel you use to capture inbound interest. The moment a prospect makes contact, the agent is already responding.

- **Monitors WhatsApp, web chat, and SMS 24/7** — no shift patterns, no out-of-office, no missed notifications
- **Responds to every enquiry within seconds** — whether it arrives at noon or midnight, the agent replies before the prospect loses interest
- **Asks qualifying questions from your sales playbook** — the agent runs friction-finding conversations designed to surface budget, timeline, and fit
- **Identifies hot prospects and books meetings** — confirmed appointments land directly in your closer's CRM calendar, not in a spreadsheet or inbox
- **Sends follow-up reminders automatically** — no prospect slips through because nobody chased them

## Training Your Lead Capture Agent

Woblar builds and trains your agent before go-live. To get your agent sounding exactly like your best salesperson, you provide:

- **Your sales playbook** — the scripts, objection-handling frameworks, and conversation flows your team already uses
- **Qualification criteria** — the specific signals that tell you a lead is worth pursuing (budget range, company size, geography, use case)
- **Your ideal customer profile** — so the agent can prioritise and triage inbound volume intelligently
- **CRM access for calendar sync** — the agent books confirmed meetings directly into your calendar so nothing requires manual data entry
