Roughly 80% of what lands in your support inbox is low-complexity, high-volume noise — store hours, password resets, order status, return policies, delivery ETAs. Your team knows the answers by heart, which is exactly why answering them manually is such a waste of their capability. Meanwhile, the high-value query from your largest account is sitting three rows below the noise, unanswered. Your Woblar Customer Support Agent handles everything in that 80% autonomously, around the clock, so your human team spends their time on the conversations that actually matter.

## Intelligent Escalation

Not every query should be resolved autonomously — and your agent knows the difference. When a conversation requires human judgment, seniority, or sensitivity, the agent escalates immediately. But it doesn't just transfer the customer and leave your team to start from scratch. Every escalation includes the full conversation history, the customer's account context, and a summary of what was already discussed. Your team picks up exactly where the agent left off. They never have to ask "what happened before this?"

This means your human agents are only handling queries that genuinely need them — and when they do, they're already fully briefed.

## Beyond FAQs: Autonomous Resolution

This is not a keyword-matching chatbot that serves pre-written responses. Your Support Agent is a real autonomous agent that takes action inside your systems. When a customer reports a payment failure at 3 AM, the agent doesn't just send a canned reply and log a ticket. It authenticates the user, queries your payment provider to identify the issue, takes the appropriate remediation action, logs the resolution to your CRM, and notifies the relevant internal contact — all within a single conversation, before your team starts their morning.

The agent works the same way your best support hire would — except it never sleeps, never has a bad day, and handles hundreds of conversations simultaneously.

## Training Your Support Agent

Woblar builds and trains your Support Agent before launch. To get your agent performing at the level of your best team member, you provide:

- **Company knowledge base** — internal wikis, product documentation, service guides, and any existing support resources your team already uses
- **FAQ document** — your most commonly asked questions and the answers you want customers to receive
- **Policies** — returns, refunds, shipping, data handling, cancellations, and any other policy your customers regularly ask about
- **Tone of voice guide** — whether your brand is formal, conversational, reassuring, or energetic, the agent matches it exactly
- **Access to relevant systems** — order management, CRM, payment platforms, and any other tools the agent needs to take action on your behalf

<Note>
  Your support agent sounds exactly like your brand — Woblar trains it on your tone of voice, not a generic template.
</Note>

<Warning>
