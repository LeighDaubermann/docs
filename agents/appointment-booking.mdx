Static web booking forms were a step forward — but they still put the friction on your customer. A form can't answer "do you have anything earlier?" at 10 PM. It can't negotiate, reassure, or collect a deposit in the same conversation. The result is half-completed bookings, no-shows, and front desk staff spending their mornings manually confirming appointments that should have confirmed themselves. Your Woblar Appointment Booking Agent handles the entire booking journey conversationally, through the channels your customers already use — WhatsApp and Instagram — at any hour of the day or night.

## How Conversational Booking Works

Your agent removes every manual step between a customer's intention to book and a confirmed slot in your calendar.

- **Customer messages on WhatsApp or Instagram at any hour** — no hold music, no "we'll get back to you during business hours"
- **Agent checks your calendar for real-time availability** — it reads your live schedule and only offers slots that are genuinely open
- **Agent confirms the booking and sends a calendar invite** — the customer receives a confirmation they can add directly to their own calendar
- **Agent requests a deposit payment if configured** — no more no-shows eating your revenue; payment is collected at the moment of booking
- **Agent sends automated reminders before the appointment** — reducing no-show rates without your team making a single phone call

## The Booking Flow: Step by Step

Here is exactly what your customer experiences from first message to confirmed appointment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Sends a Message">
    Your customer messages your WhatsApp or Instagram: "Can I book for tomorrow morning?" The agent responds within seconds, even if it's 10 PM on a Sunday.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Presents Available Slots">
    The agent reads your live calendar and replies with the available time slots for the requested period — no double-bookings, no stale data.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer Selects a Time">
    The customer picks their preferred slot in the same conversation — no redirecting to a third-party site or filling in a separate form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Confirms and Sends a Calendar Invite">
    The agent locks the booking, updates your calendar instantly, and sends the customer a confirmation with a calendar invite they can save to their phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Sends a Reminder Before the Appointment">
    24 hours before the appointment, the agent sends an automated reminder to the customer — keeping your no-show rate low without any manual follow-up from your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
