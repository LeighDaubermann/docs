Woblar is a fully managed service. You do not configure anything, log in to a dashboard, or write a single FAQ entry yourself. Woblar's team does all of that work for you — building a custom AI Agent that is trained on your specific business, deployed on the channels your customers already use, and continuously improved based on real conversation data. The result is an autonomous agent that sounds like you, knows your business, and takes meaningful action on every interaction.

## Your Private Business Brain

The foundation of every Woblar AI Agent is custom training. Generic AI models know about the world in general; your Woblar Agent knows about *your* business specifically.

During onboarding, Woblar extracts your pricing, product or service catalogue, business rules, frequently asked questions, booking policies, and the tone of voice that reflects your brand. All of this becomes the agent's knowledge base — a private, secure brain that belongs exclusively to your business. When a customer asks what your consultation fee is, or whether you have a specific product in stock, or what your cancellation policy looks like, the agent answers accurately because it was trained on the exact information you provided.

<Info>
  Your business data is never shared with other Woblar clients. Each AI Agent is isolated and trained exclusively on your information.
</Info>

Because the agent is trained on natural language rather than keyword rules, it handles questions phrased in dozens of different ways — including the ones you did not anticipate when you first shared your FAQs.

## Autonomous Actions

The most important distinction between Woblar and a simple chatbot is that your agent does not just *talk* — it *acts*. Responding with information is the floor, not the ceiling.

Depending on your business setup, your AI Agent can:

- **Qualify leads** — ask the right questions, score intent, and flag high-value prospects for your team to follow up on.
- **Book appointments** — check real-time calendar availability and write confirmed bookings directly, without any human involvement.
- **Resolve support queries** — answer questions, process straightforward requests, and close tickets end-to-end.
- **Log to your CRM** — write customer details, conversation summaries, and outcomes directly to your existing CRM so nothing gets lost.
- **Connect to your back-office systems** — push transaction data, stock queries, and customer records into your POS, ERP, or accounting software in real time, keeping your operational systems accurate without manual data entry.
- **Escalate intelligently** — recognise when a conversation needs a human and hand it off cleanly, with full context, so your team never starts from zero.

<Note>
  Woblar handles all training, maintenance, and monitoring. You don't need any technical skills — or any involvement in the day-to-day operation of your agent.
</Note>

## How a Conversation Flows

Every customer interaction follows a consistent, automated sequence from the first message to the final outcome logged in your systems.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer Sends a Message">
    A customer reaches out on WhatsApp, web chat, or Instagram — at any hour, any day. The message arrives instantly at your AI Agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Responds Instantly">
    The agent processes the message, understands the intent, and sends an accurate, on-brand response within seconds. No queue. No hold music. No missed messages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agent Takes Action">
    Depending on the conversation, the agent books an appointment, qualifies the lead, answers the support query, or progresses the customer toward the next step — autonomously.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Data Logged to Your Systems">
    Outcomes are written back to your CRM, calendar, or database in real time. Every interaction leaves a complete record your team can act on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Woblar Reviews and Tunes">
    Woblar's team analyses real conversation data regularly, identifies gaps or friction points, and updates the agent's training to make it smarter and more accurate over time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Continuous Improvement

Your AI Agent does not stay static after launch. Woblar's team monitors real customer conversations on an ongoing basis, looking for patterns that indicate the agent can be improved — questions it struggled with, topics that came up repeatedly but were not in the original training, or new products and policies you have introduced.

Based on these insights, Woblar updates the agent's knowledge base and conversational logic on a regular cadence. Every week that passes, your agent becomes a more accurate and more capable representative of your business.

<Tip>
  Keep Woblar informed when your pricing, products, or policies change. A quick email to hello@woblar.com is all it takes — Woblar handles the retraining.
</Tip>
