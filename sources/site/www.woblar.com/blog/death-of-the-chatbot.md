### The 3 AM Crisis

At 3:14 AM on a Sunday morning, a high-value B2B client attempted to process a critical $10,000 transaction through a payment gateway. It failed.

Panic set in. The client immediately went to the vendor's website, looking for help. A friendly little chat bubble popped up in the corner of the screen: _"Hi! I'm a virtual assistant. How can I help you today?"_

The client typed frantically: _"My enterprise transaction just failed, I need this cleared before the market opens in three hours."_

The chatbot, armed with the finest keyword-matching technology of 2022, replied: _"I see you are asking about 'Transactions'. Here is a link to our FAQ on how to reset your password!"_

The client closed the tab, called a competitor who offered 24/7 priority support, and moved the account by Monday morning. The original vendor didn't even know they had lost the client until the support team clocked in at 9:00 AM.

### The Chatbot Delusion

This is the reality of the "AI revolution" for most businesses. They bought into the hype, slapped a generic GPT wrapper on their website, and called it innovation. But a chatbot that can only regurgitate policy documents isn't a digital employee; it's just a slightly more annoying search bar.

Your customers don't want to chat. They want their problems solved.
