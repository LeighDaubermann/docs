### The Operational Friction

Manual data entry is the silent killer of productivity in South African finance teams. Moving invoice data from a PDF in an email to a line item in Xero is not just slow—it's highly prone to human error.

### The Impact

When your senior accountants are acting as data clerks, you are paying a massive premium for low-tier work. Furthermore, delayed invoice processing chokes cash flow visibility.

### The Shift

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) paired with LLMs can now read a PDF invoice with near-100% accuracy, categorize the expense, and push it via API to your accounting software without a human ever touching it.
