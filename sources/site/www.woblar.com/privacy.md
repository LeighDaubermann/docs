# Privacy Policy

Alien Intelligence (Pty) Ltd t/a Woblar·Effective 25 April 2026

## 1\. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to **Alien Intelligence (Pty) Ltd**, a South African private company registered under number 2023/965677/07, trading as **Woblar**(“we”, “us”, or “our”).

Woblar builds custom AI Agents for small and medium-sized businesses, delivered via WhatsApp, web chat, and Instagram integrations. Our marketing website is woblar.com.

We handle personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (“POPIA”).

## 2\. Information We Collect

### Website visitors

When you visit woblar.com we collect anonymised usage data via Google Analytics (see Section 6). This includes pages viewed, time on site, approximate geographic region, device type, and referral source. We do not collect your name or contact details simply because you browse the site.

### Contact and enquiry leads

If you fill out a contact or “Deploy” enquiry form, or email us directly, we collect your name, email address, business name, and any information you choose to share in your message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry and to follow up on a potential engagement.

### Client business data

When we build and operate an AI Agent on behalf of a client business, we may process personal information belonging to that client's end-customers (for example, names, phone numbers, order details, or support queries exchanged via WhatsApp or web chat). In this context we act as an **operator** under POPIA — we process that data on instruction from the responsible party (our client) and not for our own purposes. Our data processing agreements with clients govern these arrangements.

## 3\. How We Use Your Information

- \-To respond to enquiries and communicate with prospective or current clients.
- \-To understand how our website is used and improve its content and performance.
- \-To deliver and maintain AI Agent services we have contracted to provide.
- \-To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations under South African law.
- \-We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.
- \-We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing without your consent.

## 4\. Legal Basis for Processing

Under POPIA, we process personal information on the following grounds:

- \-**Contractual necessity:** processing required to fulfil a service agreement with a client.
- \-**Legitimate interest:** website analytics to understand and improve our service, balanced against your privacy interests.
- \-**Consent:** where you have voluntarily submitted contact information to hear from us.
- \-**Legal obligation:** where we are required to retain or disclose information by law.

## 5\. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law.

Enquiry and lead information is retained for a maximum of 24 months from last contact, after which it is securely deleted unless a formal engagement has commenced. Client engagement data is retained for the duration of the contract plus five years, in line with standard South African commercial record-keeping requirements.

Anonymised, aggregated website analytics data (collected via Google Analytics) does not contain personal information in a form that can identify you and is retained indefinitely for trend analysis.

## 6\. Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics (Google LLC, USA) to collect anonymised usage statistics. Google may transfer data to servers outside South Africa. We have enabled IP anonymisation. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

### AI model providers

Our AI Agents run on large language model providers such as Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google. When an agent handles a conversation for a client, those messages pass through these providers for processing. We only work with providers that have data protection commitments and do not allow them to use client data for model training.

### WhatsApp Business API

For WhatsApp-channel deployments, messages are transmitted through the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta Platforms, Inc.). Meta's own data processing terms and privacy policy apply to messages in transit. Our clients are responsible for ensuring their end-customers are aware that a WhatsApp Business account is operated on their behalf.

### Hosting and infrastructure

Our website is hosted on Vercel (Vercel Inc., USA). Application and agent infrastructure may run on cloud platforms including AWS or Google Cloud. These providers are required to handle data securely and have no independent access to your personal information.

- \-Object to the processing of your personal information on grounds of legitimate interest.
- \-Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.
